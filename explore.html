Top Sights in North Dakota

  1. Painted Canyon Overlook - Scenic badlands with wildlife like bison and horses.
  2. Theodore Roosevelt National Park - The national park features scenic drives and stunning landscapes.
  3. Enchanted Highway - 32-mile stretch of quaint metal sculptures.
  4. International Peace Garden - A picturesque 2,400-acre garden located on the US-Canada border.
  5. South Unit -provides a beautiful outdoor experience for both hikers and equestrians.
  6. Bottineau winter park - Ski resort with tubing, skiing and snowboarding activities.
  7. Lake Metigoshe State Park - Enjoy outdoor activities like camping, canoeing and bike trails.
  8. The Maah Daah Hey Trail - 144-mile trail perfect for outdoor enthusiasts.
  9. Lake Sakakawea - Known for its ocean-like appearance, it is the 4th largest human-made lake in the US.
  10. Roosevelt Zoo - Home to over 135 animals and is the oldest zoo in ND.
